💡 How to fill in activities

Main Activity: A Main Activity is the activity that takes most of your attention during a given time such as sleeping, eating, or working. The main activities' timeframes cannot overlap and must account for all 24 hours of your day. For example, making dinner from 5:00 PM to 5:30 PM.

Secondary Activity: A Secondary Activity is something you do at the same time as a main activity. For example, if your typical day includes cooking dinner between 5PM and 5:30PM, you would select cooking as the main activity. If you are often checking your phone while preparing dinner, you may select checking phone or tablet as the secondary action.
